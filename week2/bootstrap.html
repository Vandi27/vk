Tom and Jerry Biography

Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series that features the comedic rivalry between a cat named Tom and a mouse named Jerry. Created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, the show originally aired in the 1940s and has since become an iconic part of animation history. In this delightful cartoon, Tom constantly tries to catch Jerry, leading to hilarious chases, clever traps, and unexpected twists. The shows slapstick humor and memorable characters have entertained generations of viewers.
